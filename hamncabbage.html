Ham n Cabbage

Description

Yummy yummy yummy, get in my belly. It's a salty one bois, cabbage is usually mank but works so good with ham... just one of life's mysteries. Top off with Parsley sauce for a truly saucy dinner.

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • Cabbage

Steps

  1. Put the ham in hot water til you can eat it
  2. Put the cabbage on a pan and light the pan on fire
  3. Stick it all on a plate and throw the plate out the window
  4. Go to the pub
