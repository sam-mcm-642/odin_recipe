Fish n Chips

Description

A true classic, a big battered cod and a few chunky chipc (maybe some mushy peas too), salt n peppa and a bitta tartar sauce.

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Chips

Steps

  1. Throw a few breadcrumbs on a fish (cod maybe, ideally chop the head off first tho), and stick thre battered fish into some hot oil
  2. chopa few spuds into chips and stick the chips in oven (180 degrees c or thereabouts should do the job)
